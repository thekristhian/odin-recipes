Ingredients

  • Eggs

  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps

  • Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  • Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  • Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  • Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  • Bake until the edges are golden brown. In an oven preheated to 350 degrees F, the chocolate chip cookies should be perfectly baked in about 10 minutes

Store the cooled chocolate chip cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week.
